ZATCA Phase 2 (also called "Integration Phase") is the Saudi Arabia e-invoicing mandate requiring businesses to integrate their ERP or POS systems directly with ZATCA's Fatoora platform via API. All VAT-registered businesses in Saudi Arabia are required to comply in waves starting from January 2023. Phase 2 goes beyond Phase 1 by demanding real-time invoice reporting in UBL XML format. Rightway Systems provides a complete, certified Phase 2 integration service covering technical setup, API connectivity, and compliance testing.
