> Google Alerts is free and takes ~10 minutes to set up. Go to: **https://www.google.com/alerts**

---

## How to Set Up Each Alert

1. Go to https://www.google.com/alerts
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. Type the search string (copy exactly from the table below)
4. Click **Show options** and configure:
   - **How often:** As-it-happens (for brand/urgent) or Once a day (for everything else)
   - **Sources:** Automatic
   - **Language:** English (+ add Arabic for ZATCA if desired)
   - **Region:** Any region (global)
   - **How many:** All results
   - **Deliver to:** info@rightapps.net
5. Click **Create Alert**

## Managing Alert Fatigue

Once alerts are running, tune them after 2 weeks:
- **Too many irrelevant results?** → Refine the query (add `-site:competitor.com` to exclude, or add more specific terms)
- **No results at all?** → Broaden the query (remove quotes, try alternate terms)
- **Great results?** → Keep as-is; review monthly

### Batch Processing
- Check the **As-it-happens** alerts daily in your email
- Read the **Once-a-day** digest every morning with your daily GSC check
- Review the **Weekly** digest during your Monday morning check
