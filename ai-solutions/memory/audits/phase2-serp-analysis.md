## Executive Summary

This report analyzes Google SERPs for 8 priority keywords across two groups, identifying SERP features, content types, intent signals, AI citation eligibility, and content format recommendations for Rightway Systems to competitively rank.

**Key Finding:** The Pakistani software/IT service sector SERPs are dominated by:
1. B2B review platforms (Clutch, GoodFirms, DesignRush)
2. List articles on tech blogs (TechJuice, ProPakistani)
3. Competitor service pages (Systems Limited, Arbisoft, Folio3, Devsinc)
4. LinkedIn company results

Rightway's content strategy must target **informational** and **comparison** content to capture the top of the funnel, while service pages need schema + authority to rank for transactional terms.
